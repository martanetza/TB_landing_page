Founder story

Talent bank is a natural evolution of Projekt X, a creative agency for students, founded due to my firsthand experience of the difficulties creative & digital students had to go through when searching for jobs after graduation.

Since founding projekt X in 2018, we have accumulated a network of companies that are interested in employing students and we experienced a growing need for welcoming more students onboard. A Talent bank idea was born.

At our core, we want to facilitate talent development in collaboration with Danish companies.
